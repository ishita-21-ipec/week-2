INDIAN RESTAURANT

PAV BHAJI

Pav bhaji has many variations in ingredients and garnishes, but is essentially a spiced mixture of mashed vegetables in a thick gravy, usually cooked on a flat griddle (tava) and served hot with a soft white bread roll.

DOSA

A dosa is a rice pancake, originating from South India, made from a fermented batter. It is somewhat similar to a crepe in appearance. Its main ingredients are rice and black gram, ground together in a fine, smooth batter with a dash of salt.

CHOLE BHATURE

Chole Bhature is a spicy, tasty and yummy dish. Chole has tempting spicy flavors. Chole Bhature is originated from Northern part of India, but in Punjab, this recipe has been given a distinct twist to it taste.
